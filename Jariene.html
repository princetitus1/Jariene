A LITTLE INTRODUCTION

Welcome to J'Ariene Non-Profit & Charity Organization

J'Ariene is a registered 501(c) Nonprofit organization which goal is to transform civic
education experience for schools and communities in Liberia giving them the opportunity
to change the world around them, through mentorship and leadership development

Our Objective:

J'Ariene is focused on transforming civic education experience for students and
communities

Mission

To empowered youths and Communities civilly to be active citizens, giving them the
opportunity to change the world around them.

Collaborating Partner

J'Ariene will partner with schools and community base organizations, to implement civic
education programs

CHALLENGES
  • Lack of patriotism and citizen responsibility
  • Poor Education system
  • Unskilled and unqualified teachers
  • Out-of-school children
  • Poor infrastructure
OUR CAUSE
  • Inspire youth of their civic responsibilities and duties
  • Justice, Tolerance and Respect
  • Capacity to support and protect democracy
  • Unite and interact with government at all levels

To volunteer, kindly follow the steps below:


Choose the area of expertise. Teachers, Doctors, Nurses, Mentors, Fundraisers and,
Event Planners.


Application: Complete the volunteer application form available on our website. Provide
all relevant detail information.
Interview and screening: Once your application is received, we will contact you
to schedule an interview Orientation and Training: When and if you are selected as a volunteer, you will
participate in an orientation session where you will receive an overview of our
organization.


Volunteer Commitment: As a volunteer, we appreciate your commitment to honoring
your agreed-upon schedule and responsibilities. Kindly inform us if there are any
adjustments.
